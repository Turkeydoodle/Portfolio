Gaming

I have a passion for gaming, for many reasons that I may share with many others. I use it to detox and relax, as well as a way to entertain myself. I enjoy playing many different games, on different platforms. Some of my favorite games include Hollow Knight, Call of Duty (Warzone), and various Nintendo games.
I got into the hobby in early 2020, and have been enjoying it ever since. I have hundreds of hours inside of many different games, and have been learning about game design and mechanics. I hope to be able to expand my knowledge and skills in this area, and maybe even create a game myself one day!
