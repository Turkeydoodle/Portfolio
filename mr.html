Model Railroading

Model railroading is a fun and rewarding hobby. I enjoy building and detailing model train layouts, as well as running trains. I got into the hobby in 2020, and have been enjoying it ever since. I have built several layouts over the years, and am always looking for new ways to improve my skills and knowledge in this area.
I enjoy learning about different types of trains, track systems, and scenery techniques. I also like to attend model train shows and conventions, where I can see the latest products and meet other enthusiasts. Overall, model railroading is a great way to relax and have fun while also being creative and learning new things!
